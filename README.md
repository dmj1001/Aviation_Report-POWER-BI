# Aviation Data Report

## Overview
This project presents a comprehensive **Aviation Data Report** built in **Power BI** to monitor and optimize airline operations. The dashboard analyzes a dataset of **2,000 flights** and **450,000 passengers**, providing critical insights into flight status distributions, delay intelligence, and airline performance metrics.

## Key Features & Insights
* **Operational Overview:** High-level tracking of core KPIs, including a **56.70% On-Time Performance (OTP)** and a **6.00% Cancellation Rate**.
* **Delay Intelligence:** Detailed analysis of delay factors, showing that **33.50%** of flights experience delays, with an average delay of **43.73 minutes**.
* **Weather & Route Impact:** A specialized heatmap and impact scorecard analyzing how conditions like **Fog, Rain, and Storms** affect specific airports (e.g., DEL, BOM, MAA). Foggy conditions are shown to cause significant disruptions, averaging **51.70 minutes** of delay.
* **Airline Performance Scorecard:** Comparative rankings of major carriers including **Air India, IndiGo, Vistara, and SpiceJet**, measuring their OTP% and rank shifts by month.
* **Aircraft Efficiency:** Analysis of **Passenger Load Factors** by aircraft type, highlighting the **ATR 72** and **Boeing 737** as key performers in revenue efficiency (RASM).
* **Geographical Footprint:** Interactive **Airport Traffic Footprint** map visualizing traffic volume across the Indian subcontinent and neighboring regions.

## Technical Components
* **Business Intelligence Tool:** Power BI.
* **Visualizations Used:** * **Donut Charts:** Flight status and status mix by airline.
    * **Ribbon Charts:** Airline rank shifts by month.
    * **Waterfall Charts:** Year-Over-Year (YOY) monthly flight changes.
    * **Heatmaps:** Airport-specific weather delay matrices.
* **Advanced Metrics:** Implementation of **YOY Growth (89.75%)**, **Fuel per Passenger (5.85)**, and **Delay Severity Indexes**.

## Report Structure
* **Operations Overview:** Real-time snapshot of total flights, passenger volume, and OTP%.
* **Delay Intelligence:** Deep dive into the "why" behind flight disruptions.
* **Airline & Pilot Performance:** Benchmarking carriers against efficiency and punctuality standards.
* **Weather & Route Impact:** Analyzing seasonal trends (Monsoon, Autumn) and environmental effects on flight schedules.
